Welcome!

This is an experiment to better understand blockchain technology, and how it might begin to support the volunteer community in Calgary. Similar to sites like Three Things for Calgary this type of registry provides a public record of good deeds that people have done for their community, but with a key difference. It uses blockchain technology to store the data and to serve this page.

We're just testing things right now, but once launched this data will be permanently stored and openly accessible.
